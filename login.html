Logging into websites with Steam

A lot of the times, if you are trying to access data from steam on certain websites, you will run into login prompts that look something like the one on the right. While these usually do not take things like your password or permissions to buy or take stuff, some scammers might create websites with fake versions of these logins in order to trick users into giving them permissions without realizing.
