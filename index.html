Scams on Steam and How to Avoid Them

Steam is a platform used for sharing both video games and discussions about them. With thousands of games to choose from and millions of users, its not a suprise that it has become one of the biggest video game launchers on the internet. However, with a platform so big it is bound to have scammers on it. People will attempt to steal your accounts in order to get other games, or even access personal info. People on steam have kept track of some of the most common ways of scamming, and I will go over them here.
